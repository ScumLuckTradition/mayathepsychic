Ozzy's Arts 173 Web-Portfolio

Hello all, this webpage will act as a "Web-folio" for myself. The template I have used for this website can be found here(HTML5 UP) This page will host the art Ive done over the past year. Over the past year Ive been trying to come into my own artistically and its going fantastic, Ive been doing things that I really thought I'd never be able to do. So, as I sit on my white horse and stroke my obese felines flowing, perfect white fur, here's some artwork from yours truly.

Best of- "Unsaved Info"

This is my pièce de résistance of everything that Ive done over the past year. I find it very ironic that what I consider my best piece contradicts my own style by conveying such a peaceful mood. Maybe thats why I like it so much. What does this woman do while sitting in her bed of flowers? Not sure, but my theory is that she produces magic crystals to nuture her vines and flowers. It took my a long time to make this and it was worth it. -

  • Nothing to see here

Best of- "Doomsday Device"

This was an assignment for my Digital Media class, but its become a bit more than that. This is the piece where I really starting to understand how to stitch together an atmopshere. You dont really know what it is, and it suggests it could be dangerous since it looks very mechanical in nature. I attempted to amplify this feeling by making it seem like this device (this Doomsday Device) is partially submerged in this black liquid....maybe? Look, I dont even know what it is. And thats what adds to the atmosphere. (nothing to see here).

  • Nothing to see here

Best of-"Dont wake the Onions with your Honda, please?"

This one was a assignment in which we had to use perspective, so this is what my mind came up with in the spur of the moment. Who are these creatures? Plants I guess? Either way, the onion-trees sleep gracefully while the red devil-trees warn you not to wake their layered companions across the street. How nice of them. The textures on this piece are possibly the best ive ever made. Who's the cornflake humonculous again?
